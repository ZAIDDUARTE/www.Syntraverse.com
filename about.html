About SynTraverse Academy

Building Pakistan's digital future by training and empowering the next generation of creators, coders, and freelancers.

Our Story

SynTraverse Academy was founded with a single vision: to give young people in Pakistan access to high-demand digital skills that can help them earn independently, from anywhere. We saw a gap between traditional education and the real world, and we decided to fix it — one student at a time.

From humble beginnings to impacting dozens of lives through live mentorship, freelancing coaching, and tech bootcamps — SynTraverse is more than a training center. It's a movement.

Our Mission & Values

  • Empower youth with monetizable skills, not just certificates.
  • Offer mentorship and real-world exposure — not just lectures.
  • Create an ecosystem where students can learn, earn, and grow.
  • Make digital success accessible regardless of background.

Zaid Duarte

Founder & CEO of SynTraverse Academy

"I started SynTraverse because I believe our youth deserve more than degrees. They deserve direction, purpose, and practical skills that open global doors. We’ve trained students from zero to income — and we’re just getting started."
